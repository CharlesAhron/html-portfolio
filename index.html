Welcome

Lelouch vi Britannia is a fallen prince turned revolutionary mastermind. Armed with the mysterious power of Geass, which forces others to obey his will, he adopts the identity of Zero to wage war against the corrupt Britannian Empire. A tactical genius with unmatched charisma, Lelouch manipulates empires, commands armies, and bends the world to his vision of justice. He is both a savior and a tyrant — a legend forged in rebellion, sacrifice, and ambition.
